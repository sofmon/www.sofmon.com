Strategic open-source foundation for cloud-native startups

A robust platform designed to bootstrap the entire technology stack from zero, delivering a self-hosted, cloud-agnostic, AI-enhanced, open-source foundation, combined with strategic fractional CTO leadership for startups operating in fast-moving, high-stakes environments.

Foundation

Digital solutions are launched and scaled with confidence, built on the Sofmon foundation, which emphasizes integrated security, dynamic scalability, and regulatory compliance from inception.

Convention

A codified set of technical conventions ensures consistent, secure, and scalable operations for containerized, multi-tenant cloud-native systems. It standardizes key practices for configuration management, inter-service communication, and structured data governance.

Blueprint

A comprehensive, production-grade project template that includes fully integrated modules for API routing, secure authentication, granular authorization, payment processing, and reference implementations across mobile, web, and desktop applications.

Operations

A next-generation, AI-powered operations engine that automates and simplifies development workflows — covering everything from local environment setup and cloud infrastructure provisioning to continuous deployments and real-time performance monitoring.

Governance

Strategic oversight delivered through expert fractional CTO engagement, enabling superior architectural decisions, effective team formation, and disciplined execution of engineering processes essential to long-term startup success.

Fractional CTO

A network of seasoned CTOs with deep fintech and startup credentials brings operational excellence to early-stage ventures.

These experts define the technological groundwork, assemble lean and efficient engineering teams, and implement cost-conscious strategies that maximize impact per euro and per hire.

Engagement

A structured engagement framework that supports startups through critical phases of launch, product development, and scaling.

Each stage delivers hands-on expertise and continuous strategic input, ensuring momentum and focus are maintained.

Business model

Sofmon offers two distinct engagement models, each crafted to match the operational realities and financial structures of emerging ventures at different stages of maturity.

Pay as you go

Optimized for startups with access to capital and a need for on-demand flexibility.

Time-based billing provides immediate access to high-level CTO guidance without binding contracts, long-term commitments, or fixed overhead.

1 year 1%

Tailored for early-stage startups with limited upfront resources or those prioritizing long-term strategic alignment.

In this model, Sofmon provides a full year of CTO-level support in exchange for 1% equity, forming a deep partnership without immediate cash expenditure.
